DAY 1 — Wed Jun 17 Fly Cleveland → Anchorage ✈️ CLE → MSP → ANC ▼

Activities

  • ✈️ Delta 2847: CLE 11:00 AM → MSP 12:06 PM (Main Classic)
  • ⏳ 6-hour layover in Minneapolis — explore the airport, grab lunch, stretch your legs
  • ✈️ Delta 377: MSP 6:10 PM → ANC 8:59 PM (Comfort Classic)
  • Arrive Anchorage at 8:59 PM Alaska time (4 hours behind Ohio!)
  • Transfer to hotel, settle in for the night
  • ⚠️ Early alarm tomorrow! Train departs at 6:45 AM

Travel Day

✈️ Important: Alaska is 4 hours behind Ohio. When it's 9 PM in Anchorage, it's 1 AM back home — you'll be tired!

🌙 Fun fact: In mid-June, Anchorage gets nearly 22 hours of daylight. It won't get dark!

👪 Party of 6: Kelsie, Travis, Reagan, Michael + Cathleen & Robert

DAY 2 — Thu Jun 18 Alaska Railroad + Kenai Fjords Cruise 🚂 Anchorage → Seward + 🛳️ Cruise ▼

Activities

  • 🚂 Alaska Railroad Coastal Classic: Depart Anchorage 6:45 AM, arrive Seward 11:20 AM
  • Adventure Class seating — panoramic windows, outdoor viewing platform
  • Watch for moose, Dall sheep, and glaciers from the train!
  • Turnagain Arm coastline — one of the most scenic rail routes in the world
  • Drop bags at hotel, quick lunch in Seward
  • 🌊 Kenai Fjords & Resurrection Bay Half-Day Cruise (afternoon)
  • Wildlife: sea otters, puffins, humpback whales, Steller sea lions, bald eagles
  • Glacier viewing in Kenai Fjords National Park

Epic Double Feature!

🚂 Alaska Railroad: Booking #WRR756401 — 6 passengers, Adventure Class. Check in 1 hour early (5:45 AM). Paid: $666.

🌊 Kenai Fjords Cruise: ~4-6 hours. Dress warm — it's cold on the water even in June! Bring layers, hat, gloves.

📷 Photo ops: Glacier calving, breaching whales, puffin colonies, Exit Glacier views from the water

Where we stay:

🏨 Breeze Inn, Seward (Suite 2 Queen, Conf #31871, $398.49)
DAY 3 — Fri Jun 19 Dog Sledding + Board Ovation of the Seas! 🛷 + 🚢 Embarkation Day ▼

Activities

  • 🛷 Wilderness Dog Sled Ride & Tour (morning)
  • Meet the sled dogs, learn about mushing, ride through the Alaskan wilderness!
  • ~2-3 hours, weather dependent
  • Return to Seward, grab lunch
  • 🚢 Board Ovation of the Seas (afternoon check-in)
  • Explore the ship! 18 decks of awesomeness await
  • Find your stateroom, unpack for the week

  • First dinner onboard — dress up or keep it casual, your choice

Embarkation Day!

🛷 Dog Sledding: ~$150-200/person. Dress warm, closed-toe shoes required. The dogs are amazing!

🚢 Ship check-in: Typically 11 AM - 3 PM. Board early to maximize Day 1 at sea!

💡 Tip: Hit the Windjammer buffet for a late lunch right after boarding — it's included!

🏊 Explore the pools, FlowRider, and North Star observation pod on Day 1

Where we stay:

🚢 Ovation of the Seas (Night 1 of 7)
DAY 4 — Sat Jun 20 Hubbard Glacier — Scenic Cruising 🧊 Glacier day at sea ▼

Activities

  • 🧊 Hubbard Glacier scenic cruising — North America's largest tidewater glacier!
  • The ship cruises slowly past the 6-mile-wide face of the glacier
  • Watch for glacier calving — massive chunks of ice crashing into the sea!
  • Best viewing from your balcony, top deck, or the Two70 lounge
  • 📷 Have cameras ready — this is the #1 photo op of the trip
  • Afternoon: Explore the ship's activities
  • North Star observation pod ride (76 stories above sea level!)
  • Bumper cars at SeaPlex, FlowRider surfing, rock climbing wall
  • Evening: First formal night or casual dining

Glacier Day!

🧊 Hubbard Glacier: 76 miles long, 6 miles wide at the face. It's one of the few advancing glaciers in the world — it's actually getting bigger!

🌡️ Temperature: Expect 40-50°F near the glacier. Bundle up for outdoor viewing!

💡 Tip: The captain will announce which side of the ship has the best views. Be on deck early for prime spots.

Where we stay:

🚢 Ovation of the Seas (Night 2 of 7)
DAY 5 — Sun Jun 21 Juneau — Whale Watching + Mendenhall Glacier 🐋 Port day ▼

Activities

  • Ship docks in Juneau — Alaska's capital city (only reachable by sea or air!)
  • 🐋 Whale Watching & Mendenhall Glacier Tour (booked via Viator)
  • Whale watching: Humpback whales, orcas, Steller sea lions, harbor seals, bald eagles
  • Mendenhall Glacier: 12 miles long, Nugget Falls waterfall, visitor center
  • ~4-5 hour excursion covering both experiences
  • Free time after: Walk the Juneau waterfront, visit shops
  • Try the famous Tracy's King Crab Shack on the dock!
  • Return to ship before all-aboard time

Juneau Port Day

🐋 Excursion: Whale Watching + Mendenhall Glacier (Viator). Check meeting point — usually at the cruise port.

📍 Juneau fun fact: It's the only US state capital with no road access! You can only get here by sea or air.

🧊 Mendenhall Glacier: 12 miles long, Nugget Falls is a stunning 377-ft waterfall right at the glacier's base

💰 Tip: The Red Dog Saloon is a classic Juneau experience — sawdust floors, live music

Where we stay:

🚢 Ovation of the Seas (Night 3 of 7)
DAY 6 — Mon Jun 22 Skagway — Full Day Yukon Tour 🏔️ Port day + Yukon Territory ▼

Activities

  • Ship docks in Skagway — gateway to the Klondike Gold Rush!
  • 🏔️ Full-Day Tour of the Yukon (booked via Viator)
  • Drive over the White Pass — the famous Gold Rush route (3,292 ft)
  • Cross into Canada — bring your passport!
  • Visit Carcross Desert (world's smallest desert), Emerald Lake (stunning turquoise)
  • Stop at Yukon Suspension Bridge, Tutshi River overlook
  • ~7-8 hour full-day tour with lunch
  • Return to Skagway, quick walk through town before boarding

Yukon Adventure

🛂 PASSPORTS REQUIRED! You cross into Canada (Yukon Territory) on this tour. All 6 travelers need valid passports.

🏔️ White Pass: The narrow mountain pass that 30,000 gold rushers climbed in 1898. Now you drive it in comfort!

💎 Emerald Lake: Insanely turquoise water from glacial silt — one of the best photo ops of the trip

💡 Tip: This is a LONG day — wear comfortable shoes, bring layers, and pack snacks

Where we stay:

🚢 Ovation of the Seas (Night 4 of 7)
DAY 7 — Tue Jun 23 Endicott Arm & Dawes Glacier 🧊 Scenic cruising day ▼

Activities

  • 🧊 Endicott Arm Fjord & Dawes Glacier scenic cruising
  • 30-mile fjord lined with 1,000-ft walls of rock and ice
  • Icebergs floating in the water — watch for harbor seals lounging on them!
  • Dawes Glacier at the end of the fjord — active calving glacier
  • Ship's naturalist narrates the wildlife and geology
  • Afternoon: Relax and enjoy the ship
  • Kids: bumper cars, basketball court, video arcade, rock wall
  • Adults: spa, casino, live shows in the Royal Theatre
  • Evening: Enjoy one of the specialty restaurants (Giovanni's, Chops, etc.)

Fjord Day

🧊 Endicott Arm: Less crowded than Tracy Arm but equally stunning. The fjord narrows dramatically — it feels like the ship barely fits!

🐻 Wildlife: Harbor seals on icebergs, mountain goats on cliffs, bald eagles, brown bears on shore

🎭 Tonight: Check the Cruise Compass for tonight's shows — Ovation has incredible entertainment

Where we stay:

🚢 Ovation of the Seas (Night 5 of 7)
DAY 8 — Wed Jun 24 Ketchikan — Totem Poles & Creek Street 🏞️ Port day ▼

Activities

  • Ship docks in Ketchikan — the “Salmon Capital of the World”
  • No excursion booked — explore on your own!
  • 🏛️ Totem Heritage Center — world's largest collection of original totem poles
  • 🏠 Creek Street — boardwalk built on stilts over Ketchikan Creek. Watch salmon jumping!
  • 🐟 Great Alaskan Lumberjack Show — axe throwing, log rolling, tree climbing competition
  • Walk through downtown — colorful buildings, art galleries, smoked salmon shops
  • Grab fresh fish & chips at a local spot
  • Return to ship for departure

Ketchikan Highlights

🌧️ Be prepared: Ketchikan gets 150+ inches of rain per year — bring your rain jacket!

🐟 Fun fact: During salmon season (Jul-Sep), you can watch salmon literally jumping up the fish ladder at Deer Mountain Hatchery. June may be early but check!

🛍️ Shopping: Ketchikan is great for Alaskan souvenirs — smoked salmon, native art, jade jewelry

💰 Free activities: Creek Street, fish hatchery, and walking downtown are all free!

Where we stay:

🚢 Ovation of the Seas (Night 6 of 7)

Activities

  • 🌊 Cruising south through the Inside Passage
  • Last full day to enjoy the ship — make it count!
  • Kids: hit every activity you haven't tried yet

🧳 Packing tip: Set luggage outside your stateroom door by 10 PM tonight. Keep a small bag with essentials for tomorrow.

📷 Photo tip: Take family photos on the ship today — last chance with the Alaska backdrop!

💰 Money-Saving Tips

  • • Book excursions through Viator — often 20-40% cheaper than cruise ship excursions
  • • Ketchikan: No excursion needed! Creek Street, totem poles, and downtown are free
  • • Drink package math: Only worth it if you average 5+ drinks/day (do the math for your trip)
  • • WiFi: Buy the cruise ship WiFi package before you board — it's cheaper pre-cruise
  • • Specialty dining: Book lunch instead of dinner at specialty restaurants — same food, lower price
  • • Gratuities: ~$16/person/day are auto-charged. Budget ~$672 total for 6 people, 7 nights.
